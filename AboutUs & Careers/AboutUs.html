About Us

Film Society was founded in 1910 by Tiberiu Rociu, intially his vision started as a simple theatre building, but as our vison grew, so does our bussines, we have now expanded our across four key markets,acting, production, hosting events and Cinematography.

From 1990 until 1999 Film Society was stricly in the acting business, after that time the company extended and Film Society is now the best and most modern theatre and event hosting company in Scotland, with multiple awards from accredited sources. All ten of our Special Cinema Rooms benefit from the new Sony 8K technology and 4 theatre Rooms that have the latest playback capabilities, noise cancellation, comfortable seats and much more. The modern design, the latest technology as well as the excellent positioning in the city centre, will make Film Society the favourite destination for watching movies or taking part in a hosting events.
