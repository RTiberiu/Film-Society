Careers

Here at Film Society we offer talented individuals the career experience of their dreams, whether you are a seasoned actor, a film maker or a ticket seller, Film Society offers a wide range of opportunities for the right people.

We mainly focus on supporting upcoming stars in their journey, film making and production, hosting plays and movies, we are also proud and known for our cinema experience. If you are right candidate for the job, Film Society is the place for you, you will be delighted to know that we offer a very competitive salary, career progression, health insurance, meal breaks, work and life balance, and many other perks.

Career progression explained

We are extremely proud of our career progression system here at Film Society, if you are the candidate we are looking for,you should know that we offer a very special progression opportunity, that consists of a training tier system that helps you move up the ladder. We stand for values and we honour our employees and customers, for example, we offer all kinds of training opportunities for our employees, courses that would help you progress from the Movie Set Crew role to a Movie Set Camera-man Trainee, or from the Front Desk Ticket Seller to the Front Desk Manager.

What are we looking for?

The perfect candidate will have to meet our criteria for his job description along with a certain code of ethics we stand for: Competency Creativity Positivity Honesty
